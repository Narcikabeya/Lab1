Low and Slow

Posted by BGB,November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide.In souds vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precidely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.
